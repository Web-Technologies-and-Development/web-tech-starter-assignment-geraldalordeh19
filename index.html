Shadow Protocol

Release Date: December 15, 2026

About the Movie

In a world where digital surveillance controls every aspect of life, a former intelligence agent discovers a hidden system known as the Shadow Protocol. This system has the power to manipulate global events from behind the scenes. As he dives deeper, he realizes that the truth is more dangerous than the lies people have been told for years. With enemies closing in and allies turning against him, he must uncover the truth before the system erases him completely. The fate of the world depends on one final decision—expose the truth or become part of the system forever.

Cast

  • Alex Kane – A rogue agent trying to uncover hidden truths.
  • Lena Voss – A cyber expert who helps decode the system.
  • Director Hale – The mysterious leader behind the protocol.
